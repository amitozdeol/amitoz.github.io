Step In, Speak Up!

Understand the challenges that LGBTQ students face and practice techniques for creating a supportive environment by engaging in role-play scenarios with virtual students. After completing the simulation, you will be better equipped to: recognize the needs and obstacles of LGBTQ students; promote a “safe space” with effective classroom management techniques; initiate a conversation about sensitive topics, such as bullying; know when and how to ask a student if they are thinking about suicide.
